A compounding pharmacy that custom make medications

Compounding is the art ans science of creating personalized medicine in order to fit unique need of patients.

Many patients aren't served by mass-produced medications. The needs sometimes start when a child who can't swallow pills; a patient who has gluten or lactose intolerance; a patient who can't tolerate the side effects from an oral medication; a medicine that is not commercially available. Compounding pharmacy can provide solutions for these issues.
